Our History

  • The Center forI nternational Education, Inc. is founded.

  • 1989

    NC Department of Public Instruction (DPI)requires world language learning for all students.

  • 1990

    20 years later the world's first web sites arrive.

  • 1992

    Netscape, Yahoo and Amazon launch.

  • 1994

    The 1,000,000th domain launches. Time Magazine announces, "Welcome to the wired world."

  • 1995

    Dot-com boom peaks. PHP4.0 Released.

  • 1998-1999

    More and more website launches and Drupal 1.0 released

  • 2000-2001

    Wifi goes mainstream.

  • 2001-2002

    Web 2.0: The internet becomes a two-way street.Businesses demand tools to manage their sites.

  • 2003-2004

    iPhone 1 is released. Local development tools appear.
